FMMO Class IV formula

Applied monthly with CME NFDM spot avg and NASS butter avg

Class IV = (Skim × 0.965) + (BFat × 3.5)
Skim = ((NFDM − $0.1678) × 0.99) × 9
BFat = (Butter − $0.1715) × 1.211
Make allowances: NFDM $0.1678/lb · Butter $0.1715/lb · Yields: Skim ×9 · BFat ×1.211
